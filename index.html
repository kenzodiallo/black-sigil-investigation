Purpose

This hub serves as a collection point for raw information, data, and references to support the main Black Sigil Investigation site. Contributors can submit findings, notes, and sources here in a structured and verifiable manner.

How it works

  • Submit raw OSINT data as Markdown posts
  • Include source, description, and verification notes
  • Information here can be processed and published on the main site
  • All contributions are versioned via Git commits

Contribute

You can help Black Sigil Investigation by submitting information or following our contribution guidelines:

  • Submit Information (via secure email or GitHub)
  • Read Contribution Guidelines

All contributions are reviewed manually. Nothing is published automatically.

Current Status

This collection hub is in the initial research phase. Posts will accumulate progressively and be organized by date and topic.

Disclaimer

This hub is intended for informational and research purposes only. No classified, illegal, or private data is collected or disclosed. Readers and contributors are encouraged to verify sources independently.
